STEP 1: Login

→ Enter: admin@example.com / admin123
→ Click "Sign In"

STEP 2: Access Admin Features
→ Navigate to Papers/Notes/Syllabus sections
→ You'll see GREEN "Add" buttons at the top
→ Click to open upload form

STEP 3: Upload Content
→ Fill in the form:

→ Click to delete individual items
→ OR use checkboxes + "Delete Selected" for bulk delete
